Common Household Plumbing Problems and DIY Solutions

March 15, 2025

Discover essential solutions for common household plumbing problems in this comprehensive guide. From dripping faucets wasting thousands of liters annually to clogged drains and running toilets consuming up to 200 gallons daily, learn effective DIY fixes for these persistent issues. The guide covers simple solutions like replacing washers, using natural drain cleaners, and adjusting toilet components. While many problems can be handled independently, some situations demand professional intervention, including frozen pipes and sewer backups. Regular maintenance tips, such as inspecting pipes and using drain strainers, help prevent future plumbing headaches. Know when to DIY and when to call experts.
