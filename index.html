About Calgary Plumbing

Founded in 2000, Calgary Plumbing has grown from a small family business to Calgary's leading plumbing and heating service provider. Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide honest, reliable plumbing services to our community. Through dedication to excellence and customer satisfaction, we've built a reputation as the most trusted name in Calgary's plumbing industry.

Our core values of integrity, expertise, and customer-first service drive everything we do. We believe in transparent pricing, continuous training for our team, and using the latest technology to deliver superior results. As active members of the Calgary community, we're committed to environmental sustainability and giving back through various local initiatives.

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas of Calgary do you service?

We provide comprehensive plumbing and heating services throughout Calgary and surrounding areas, including Airdrie, Cochrane, Chestermere, and Okotoks.

How quickly can you respond to emergencies?

Our emergency response team typically arrives within 60 minutes of your call, 24/7, 365 days a year. We prioritize urgent issues to prevent property damage.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit, e-transfer, and cash. We also offer flexible payment plans for larger projects and can provide direct billing to insurance companies.

Do you offer warranties on your work?

Yes, we provide a comprehensive warranty on all our work. Parts and labor are typically covered for 1-2 years, depending on the service provided.

Are your plumbers licensed and insured?

All our plumbers are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. They undergo regular training and certification updates to stay current with the latest industry standards.
